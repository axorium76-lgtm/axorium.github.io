What is a Fuel Cost Calculator?

A Fuel Cost Calculator helps users estimate the fuel consumption and cost for a trip based on distance, vehicle mileage, and fuel price.

How the Calculation Works

The calculator divides the distance by the vehicle’s mileage to find liters of fuel required, then multiplies by the fuel price to get the total cost.

Example

If your trip is 500 km, your vehicle mileage is 20 km/l, and fuel price is ₹100/l, the calculator shows: Fuel Needed = 25 L, Total Cost = ₹2500.

Why This Tool is Useful

This tool saves time and helps in budgeting fuel expenses for trips, daily commutes, or logistics planning.
