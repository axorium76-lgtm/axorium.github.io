What is a Win Rate Calculator?

A Win Rate Calculator helps traders determine the percentage of trades that were profitable compared to the total number of trades executed.

How Win Rate Works

The win rate measures trading accuracy. It compares winning trades against the total number of trades including both wins and losses.

Example

If a trader makes 60 winning trades and 40 losing trades, the win rate would be 60%.

Why This Tool is Useful

Understanding win rate helps traders evaluate the effectiveness of their trading strategy and improve their risk management decisions.
