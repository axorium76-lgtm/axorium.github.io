What is an EMI Prepayment Calculator?

An EMI Prepayment Calculator helps borrowers understand how much interest they can save by making an early payment toward their loan principal. It is commonly used for home loans, car loans, and personal loans.

How EMI Prepayment Works

When you prepay a portion of your loan principal, the outstanding loan amount reduces. This lowers the interest calculated on the remaining balance, which ultimately reduces the total interest payable.

Example

If you take a loan of ₹10,00,000 for 10 years at 8% interest and make a prepayment of ₹1,00,000, the calculator will show how much interest you save over the remaining tenure.

Why This Tool is Useful

This calculator helps borrowers plan smarter loan repayment strategies and reduce the overall cost of borrowing by making timely prepayments.
