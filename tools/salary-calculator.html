What is a Salary Calculator?

A Salary Calculator is a financial tool used to calculate net salary after accounting for tax deductions, HRA, and allowances. It shows how much an employee actually takes home.

How the Calculation Works

The calculator adds basic salary, HRA, and allowances to get the gross salary. It then calculates tax deduction based on the provided percentage and subtracts it from gross salary to display net salary.

Example

If your basic salary is ₹50,000, HRA ₹10,000, allowance ₹5,000, and tax deduction is 10%, the calculator will show: Gross Salary = ₹65,000, Tax Deduction = ₹6,500, Net Salary = ₹58,500.

Why This Tool is Useful

A Salary Calculator helps employees, freelancers, and business owners quickly calculate take-home pay and understand deductions. It prevents manual errors and saves time in financial planning.
