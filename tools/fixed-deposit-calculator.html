What is a Fixed Deposit Calculator?

A Fixed Deposit Calculator helps investors calculate the maturity amount and interest earned on a fixed deposit investment. It allows users to estimate returns before investing in a bank or financial institution.

How Fixed Deposit Calculation Works

The maturity value of a fixed deposit depends on the principal amount, the interest rate offered by the bank, and the duration of the investment. The calculator uses compound interest to determine the final amount.

Example

If you invest ₹1,00,000 at an interest rate of 7% for 5 years, the maturity value will be approximately ₹1,40,255, meaning you earn around ₹40,255 in interest.

Why This Tool is Useful

A Fixed Deposit Calculator allows investors to compare different interest rates and durations to maximize their returns. It is widely used by individuals planning safe and stable investments.
