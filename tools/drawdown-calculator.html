What is a Drawdown Calculator?

A Drawdown Calculator measures the percentage decline in a trading account from its highest value to its lowest value before recovery.

Why Drawdown Matters

Drawdown shows the risk level of a trading strategy. Lower drawdowns indicate better risk management.

Example

If your trading account reached ₹1,00,000 and later dropped to ₹75,000, your drawdown would be 25%.

How Traders Use It

Professional traders track drawdown to ensure their strategy maintains sustainable risk levels over time.
