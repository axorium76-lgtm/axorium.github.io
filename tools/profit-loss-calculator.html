What is a Profit & Loss Calculator?

A Profit & Loss Calculator helps traders and business owners determine the profit or loss on a trade or transaction. By entering the buying price, selling price, and quantity, the calculator instantly shows whether a trade is profitable or results in a loss.

How This Tool Works

The calculator uses a simple formula: Profit/Loss = (Selling Price - Buying Price) × Quantity. After entering the purchase price, selling price, and number of units or shares, it calculates the total profit or loss and can also show the percentage gain or loss for better decision-making.

Example

For example, if a trader buys 100 shares at ₹50 each and sells them at ₹60 each, the profit & loss calculator will show a total profit of ₹1,000 and a 20% gain.

Why Use a Profit & Loss Calculator?

A profit & loss calculator helps traders and businesses quickly assess the outcome of their transactions without performing manual calculations. It ensures accuracy, saves time, and helps make informed financial decisions.
