What is a SIP Calculator?

A SIP (Systematic Investment Plan) Calculator helps investors estimate the future value of their regular investments in mutual funds or other investment instruments. It shows how much your monthly contributions can grow over time based on the expected rate of return.

How This Tool Works

The calculator requires inputs such as monthly investment amount, expected annual return rate, and investment duration. Using the power of compounding, it calculates the total corpus you can accumulate by the end of the investment period.

Example

For example, if someone invests ₹10,000 per month for 10 years in a mutual fund with an expected annual return of 12%, the SIP calculator will estimate the total investment value at the end of 10 years, including the profit earned through compounding.

Why Use a SIP Calculator?

A SIP calculator helps investors plan their long-term financial goals and understand the potential growth of their investments. It provides clarity on how much to invest regularly to achieve a target corpus, making financial planning simpler and more effective.
