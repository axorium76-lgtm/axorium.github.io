What is a Position Risk Calculator?

A Position Risk Calculator helps traders determine the potential loss if their stop loss is triggered during a trade.

How Position Risk Works

The calculator measures the difference between the entry price and stop loss price and multiplies it by the position size.

Example

If a trader enters at ₹100, sets a stop loss at ₹95, and buys 100 shares, the total risk would be ₹500.

Why This Tool is Useful

This tool helps traders control losses and manage risk before entering a trade.
