What is a Percentage Change Calculator?

A Percentage Change Calculator determines the percentage increase or decrease between two values.

How It Works

The calculator compares a new value with the original value and expresses the difference as a percentage.

Example

If a stock price increases from ₹100 to ₹120, the percentage increase is 20%.

Why This Tool is Useful

This calculator is widely used in finance, investing, economics, and business analysis.
