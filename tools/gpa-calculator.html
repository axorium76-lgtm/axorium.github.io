What is a GPA Calculator?

A GPA Calculator is a tool that helps students calculate their Grade Point Average based on the grades they receive in different subjects or courses. It converts letter grades or percentage scores into grade points and then computes the average to show the overall academic performance.

How This Tool Works

The calculator requires inputs such as subject grades and sometimes credit hours for each course. It converts the grades into numerical grade points and calculates the average based on the total points and total credits. The result is the student's GPA for a semester or academic period.

Example

For example, if a student receives grades A, B, and A in three subjects, with each subject having equal credit, the GPA calculator will convert these grades into grade points and calculate an average GPA such as 3.67 depending on the grading scale used.

Why Use a GPA Calculator?

A GPA calculator helps students quickly understand their academic standing without doing manual calculations. It is useful for tracking academic progress, planning study goals, and checking eligibility for scholarships or academic programs.
