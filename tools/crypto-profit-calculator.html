What is a Crypto Profit Calculator?

A Crypto Profit Calculator helps traders calculate the profit or loss made from cryptocurrency trades. It uses the buy price, sell price, and quantity to provide instant results.

How the Calculation Works

The calculator subtracts the buy price from the sell price, multiplies by the quantity, and displays whether you made a profit or a loss.

Example

If you bought 2 BTC at ₹20,00,000 each and sold at ₹22,00,000, the calculator shows: Profit = ₹4,00,000.

Why This Tool is Useful

This tool is crucial for crypto traders to quickly analyze trades, understand gains or losses, and plan future trading strategies.
