What is a Risk Per Trade Calculator?

A Risk Per Trade Calculator helps traders determine the maximum amount of money they should risk on a single trade based on their trading capital and risk percentage.

Why Risk Management Matters

Professional traders protect their capital by limiting the amount they risk on each trade. This prevents large losses that could damage the trading account.

Example

If your trading account is ₹1,00,000 and you risk 2% per trade, the maximum risk allowed per trade would be ₹2,000.

Why This Tool is Useful

This calculator helps traders maintain disciplined risk management and avoid emotional or oversized trades.
