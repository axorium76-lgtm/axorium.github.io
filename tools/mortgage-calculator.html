What is a Mortgage Calculator?

A Mortgage Calculator helps you estimate your monthly mortgage payments based on the loan amount, interest rate, and loan term. It allows homeowners and prospective buyers to understand the cost of a home loan before borrowing.

How This Tool Works

The calculator takes the principal loan amount, annual interest rate, and loan tenure as inputs. Using the standard mortgage formula, it calculates the fixed monthly payment and, in some versions, can also provide a detailed payment schedule showing how much goes toward principal and interest over time.

Example

For example, if someone takes a mortgage of ₹50,00,000 at an interest rate of 7% for 20 years, the mortgage calculator will estimate the monthly payment and may also show a breakdown of payments for each year, helping the borrower understand how the loan balance decreases over time.

Why Use a Mortgage Calculator?

A mortgage calculator helps homebuyers plan their finances and choose affordable loan options. It provides quick insights into monthly payments, total interest cost, and loan affordability without manually calculating complex formulas.
