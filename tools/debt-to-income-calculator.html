What is a Debt-to-Income Ratio Calculator?

A Debt-to-Income (DTI) Ratio Calculator helps individuals determine how much of their monthly income goes toward paying debts. Lenders use this ratio to evaluate whether a borrower can handle additional loan payments.

How Debt-to-Income Ratio Works

The ratio compares total monthly debt obligations with total monthly income. A lower ratio means better financial stability and higher chances of loan approval.

Example

If your monthly income is ₹60,000 and your total monthly debt payments are ₹15,000, your debt-to-income ratio would be 25%.

Why This Tool is Useful

This calculator helps individuals understand their financial health and determine whether they are in a good position to apply for a loan or reduce existing debt.
