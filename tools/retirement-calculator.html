What is a Retirement Calculator?

A Retirement Calculator helps you estimate how much money you will need to save to maintain your desired lifestyle after retirement. It takes into account your current savings, expected retirement age, monthly contributions, and expected rate of return to project your retirement corpus.

How This Tool Works

The calculator uses inputs such as current age, retirement age, current savings, monthly or yearly contributions, and expected annual growth rate of investments. It calculates the total amount you will accumulate by retirement and may also estimate how long your savings will last based on your expected expenses.

Example

For example, if someone is 35 years old with ₹5,00,000 in savings, plans to contribute ₹10,000 monthly, and expects a 7% annual return, the retirement calculator will estimate the total retirement corpus at age 60 and show if it meets their financial goals.

Why Use a Retirement Calculator?

A retirement calculator helps individuals plan for the future by understanding how much to save and invest to achieve a comfortable retirement. It makes financial planning easier and ensures that you stay on track with your long-term goals.
