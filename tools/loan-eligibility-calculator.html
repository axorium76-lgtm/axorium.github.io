What is a Loan Eligibility Calculator?

A Loan Eligibility Calculator helps individuals estimate how much loan amount they may qualify for based on their income, interest rate, and loan tenure.

How Loan Eligibility is Calculated

Most banks allow a certain percentage of a borrower's monthly income to be used for EMI payments. This calculator estimates the loan amount based on an EMI affordability rule and the chosen loan tenure and interest rate.

Example

If your monthly income is ₹50,000 and you can afford an EMI of ₹25,000, the calculator estimates the loan amount you may qualify for depending on the interest rate and loan tenure.

Why This Tool is Useful

This calculator helps borrowers plan their finances and understand their borrowing capacity before applying for a home loan, car loan, or personal loan.
