What is a Lot Size Calculator?

A Lot Size Calculator helps traders determine the correct position size based on their account balance, risk tolerance, and stop loss distance.

Why Lot Size Matters

Trading with the correct lot size ensures that traders maintain proper risk management and avoid excessive losses.

Example

If a trader has ₹1,00,000 in their account and risks 2% per trade with a stop loss of 50 points, the calculator estimates the optimal lot size.

Who Should Use It?

Forex traders, stock traders, and crypto traders can use this tool to maintain disciplined position sizing.
