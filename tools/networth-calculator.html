What is a Net Worth Calculator?

A Net Worth Calculator is a financial tool that calculates your net worth by subtracting total liabilities from total assets. It gives a clear picture of your financial standing.

How the Calculation Works

The calculator adds up all your assets (cash, investments, property) and subtracts all your liabilities (loans, debts, mortgages) to compute net worth.

Example

If your total assets are ₹10,00,000 and liabilities are ₹4,00,000, the calculator will show: Net Worth = ₹6,00,000.

Why This Tool is Useful

A Net Worth Calculator helps individuals understand their financial health, plan investments, and track progress over time.
