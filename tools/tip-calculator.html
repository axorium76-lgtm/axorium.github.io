What is a Tip Calculator?

A Tip Calculator is a simple financial tool used to calculate the tip amount for a bill and the total bill including tip. It helps avoid manual calculations and ensures accurate tipping.

How the Calculation Works

The calculator multiplies the bill amount by the tip percentage and divides by 100 to get the tip amount. It then adds this tip to the bill to display the total payable amount.

Example

If your bill is ₹1,000 and you want to tip 10%, the calculator will show: Tip Amount = ₹100, Total Bill = ₹1,100.

Why This Tool is Useful

A Tip Calculator saves time, prevents mistakes, and ensures correct tipping in restaurants, hotels, or delivery services.
