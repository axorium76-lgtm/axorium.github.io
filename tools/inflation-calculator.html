What is an Inflation Calculator?

An Inflation Calculator helps estimate how the value of money changes over time due to inflation. It shows how much a certain amount of money from the past would be worth today or how much money you may need in the future to maintain the same purchasing power.

How This Tool Works

The calculator uses an inflation rate and a time period to determine the change in purchasing power. By entering an initial amount, the starting year, and the ending year, the tool calculates the adjusted value based on the rate of inflation.

Example

For example, if someone had ₹10,000 in the year 2010 and the average annual inflation rate was 6%, the inflation calculator would estimate how much money would be required today to buy the same goods and services.

Why Use an Inflation Calculator?

An inflation calculator helps people understand the long-term effects of rising prices on their savings and investments. It is useful for financial planning, retirement planning, and understanding how the value of money changes over time.
