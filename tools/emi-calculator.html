What is an EMI Calculator?

An EMI Calculator helps you calculate the Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) you need to pay when taking a loan. It shows the fixed monthly amount you must pay to repay a loan over a specific period with interest. This tool is commonly used for home loans, car loans, personal loans, and education loans.

How This Tool Works

The calculator uses three main inputs: loan amount, interest rate, and loan tenure. By applying the standard EMI formula, the tool calculates the monthly installment that includes both the principal repayment and interest amount. Once the values are entered, the calculator instantly displays the EMI you need to pay every month.

Example

For example, if someone takes a loan of ₹5,00,000 at an annual interest rate of 10% for a period of 5 years, the EMI calculator will estimate a monthly payment of approximately ₹10,624 depending on the exact calculation method.

Why Use an EMI Calculator?

An EMI calculator helps borrowers plan their finances before taking a loan. It allows users to compare different loan amounts, interest rates, and repayment periods to choose the most affordable option without doing complex manual calculations.
