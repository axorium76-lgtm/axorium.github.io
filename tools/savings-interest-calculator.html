What is a Savings Interest Calculator?

A Savings Interest Calculator helps individuals estimate the interest earned on money kept in a savings account over a specific period.

How Savings Interest Works

Banks pay interest on the balance maintained in savings accounts. This calculator estimates the interest earned based on the deposit amount, interest rate, and time period.

Example

If you keep ₹50,000 in a savings account with an annual interest rate of 4% for 2 years, the calculator shows the interest earned and the final balance.

Why This Tool is Useful

This calculator helps users understand how their savings grow over time and plan better financial decisions.
