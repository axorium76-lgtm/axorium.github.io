What is a Tax Calculator?

A Tax Calculator is a financial tool used to calculate the tax amount you owe based on your income and tax rate. It instantly shows how much tax you need to pay and the net income after tax.

How the Calculation Works

The calculator multiplies your income by the tax rate (in percentage) and divides by 100. This gives the tax amount. Then it subtracts the tax from your income to show your net income.

Example

If your income is ₹50,000 and the tax rate is 10%, the calculator will show ₹5,000 as Tax Amount and ₹45,000 as Net Income After Tax.

Why This Tool is Useful

A Tax Calculator saves time, prevents mistakes, and helps you plan your finances. It is useful for individuals, freelancers, and small business owners to know their tax obligations instantly.
